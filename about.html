A little about us

Who are we?

KewlHost was founded in November 2018, with its original team from an online community named Kewl. The team were all vivid gamers, and as was the community. So we found we all had one thing in common - the need for game-servers to play with each other. With the original intentions providing everyone with servers free of charge, we quickly grew and evolved into something else. KewlHost is a game-server host made by gamers, for gamers - so we understand what is most important.

Goal

Our aim is to be the perfect place for gamers to go and purchase servers. We try to provide as many precious features as possible and make our services available to anyone by making our service affordable. KewlHost not only wants whats best for its customers, but the environment and people who are less unfortunate than us - who might not be able to enjoy games because of a condition. So we promote environmentally friendly methods, and donate 10% of all income to charity.
